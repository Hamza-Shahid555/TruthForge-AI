File a claim below and watch it get proof-tested for real — struck from opposite sides until the Judge can certify what holds.

Two LLM agents research your claim in parallel — one arguing for it, one against — drawing on a local evidence corpus and live web search. A judge scores every round with a structured ruling. When the judge genuinely cannot decide, the graph pauses and waits for you.
